xlinked, ylinked: x, y coordinates of moving tracks (ie ones that were not filtered out as being stationary)
xlinked_before, ylinked_before: x, y coordinates of all tracks
totdelx_all, totdely_all: total background drift
conv: conversion in microns/pixel
filename='t'+zero-indexed position on 96-well plate (across rows then columns, i.e. 0=A1, 1=A2, ..., 12=B1, etc)
foldername=plate index (ex. DE1 or mreB) + replicate (a, b, c, d)
